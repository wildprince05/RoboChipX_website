About ROBOCHIPX

Empowering Future Tech Innovators

ROBOCHIPX 2026 is an intense 24-hour innovation incubator that bridges the gap between hardware architecture, robotic design, and VLSI circuit systems. We welcome students from all engineering backgrounds to push boundaries, collaborate, and engineer solutions that matter.

Whether you are specialized in hardware descriptive languages (HDLs), embedded systems, or autonomous path planning, you will find a home and high-stakes competition right here.

24H Action

Non-stop coding, routing, modeling, and real-time hardware debugging.

Silicon Forge

Design cutting-edge architectures using advanced simulation suites.

Robotic Arenas

Hands-on robotics labs for prototyping, testing, and system integration.

Top Mentors

Industry-certified jury members from silicon developers and robotics firms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to participate?
All undergraduate engineering and technology students (B.E / B.Tech / M.E / M.Tech) from all institutions are eligible to participate.
What is the team configuration?
Upto 4 members per team.
Is hardware/software provided?
Teams must carry their own components and all other required items.
Will accommodation be provided?
Yes, resting zones, facilities for grooming, and meals (only night) will be provided to all registered participants within the campus throughout the 24 hours.
