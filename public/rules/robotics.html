Rules & Regulations

  Back
  1. Each team must have 2 to 4 members. Intercollege members are allowed.
  2. The hackathon runs for 24 continuous hours. At least one or two members must stay at the venue during the overnight session.
  3. Bring your own laptops, chargers, robot components, sensors, actuators, and tools.
  4. Any microcontroller, development board, motor driver, servo, or robotics platform is allowed.
  5. All robots and hardware must follow the safety guidelines given by the organizers.
  6. Every team must present a functional robot or working robotic prototype.
  7. A PPT using the official organizer template is mandatory.
  8. Be ready to walk the judges through your robot's design, how it works, and the logic behind it.
  9. Keep it original — copying or unsafe behavior may get your team disqualified.
💡 Open Innovation
You can also bring your own project idea from this track. No problem statement needed — just build something amazing!
